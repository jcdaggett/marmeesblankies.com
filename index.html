Good to Know

Questions, answered

Are the blankets machine washable?

Yes. Machine wash cold on gentle cycle. Tumble dry low or lay flat. Do not bleach. Do not iron directly on the minky.

What are the blankets made of?

Two soft layers sewn back to back: combinations of cotton flannel, cuddle minky, sherpa, fleece, canvas, linen, and other quality fabrics, most finished with a smooth satin edge. Each one is double-sided and fully reversible.

What sizes do you offer?

Every ready-made blanket is one of a kind with its own exact measurements, listed on the product. As a general guide: Changing Pad blankets run about 17″–21″ × 21″–29″, Travel blankets about 22″–25″ × 29″–35″, and Cuddle blankets about 31″–34″ × 30″–43″. Custom sizing is coming soon with our made-to-order launch.

Is each blanket really one of a kind?

Yes. Every blanket is sewn one at a time by Marmee herself — no factory line. When a ready-made blanket sells, that exact combination is gone for good.

Can I order a custom blanket?

Very soon. Made-to-order blankets — your fabrics, your size — are launching shortly. Use the "Notify Me at Launch" button above and you'll be first to know.

Where are the blankets made?

Every blanket is handmade in Texas and ships anywhere in the US.

Why do Marmee's blankets cost more than store-bought blankets?

Because they're simply better blankets. Every Marmee's blanket is thoughtfully designed using high-quality fabrics chosen not just for how they look, but for how they feel together. Combined with durable construction and careful hand-finishing, the result is exceptional softness, lasting durability, and a blanket that's made to be loved for years.

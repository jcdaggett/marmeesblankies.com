Our Story

Made the way they used to be.

Marmee's Blankies started the way the best things do — with one blanket, made for someone loved. What began as a gift became a calling.

Every blanket is layered by hand: a soft flannel print on one side, thick plush minky on the other, sewn one at a time. No factory line, no two exactly alike — just blankets made to be wrapped up in and handed down.

— Marmee

Good to Know

Questions, answered

Are the blankets machine washable?

Yes. Every blanket is made to be used, not babied — wash cold on gentle, tumble dry low or hang dry. The minky and faux fur stay soft through years of washing.

What are the blankets made of?

Two soft layers sewn back to back: combinations of plush minky, deep faux fur, and quality printed fabrics, most finished with a smooth satin edge. Each one is double-sided and fully reversible.

What sizes do you offer?

Baby Cuddle (30″ × 44″) for newborns and toddlers, and Lap (45″ × 60″) for kids and adults — couch, recliner, game day. Custom sizes are coming soon with our made-to-order launch.

Is each blanket really one of a kind?

Yes. Every blanket is sewn one at a time by Marmee herself — no factory line. When a ready-made blanket sells, that exact combination is gone for good.

Can I order a custom blanket?

Very soon. Made-to-order blankets — your fabrics, your size, optional embroidered name — are launching shortly. Use the "Notify Me at Launch" button above and you'll be first to know.

Where are the blankets made?

Every blanket is handmade in Texas and ships anywhere in the US.
